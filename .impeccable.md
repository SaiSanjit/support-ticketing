## Design Context

### Users
Support agents and managers who need to track and resolve infrastructure alerts and support tickets efficiently. The context is professional, high-stakes (critical alerts), and requires clarity and quick action.

### Brand Personality
- **Professional**: Reliability and trust.
- **Data-Forward**: Clear presentation of complex information.
- **Premium**: High-end feel, editorial-style layouts, sophisticated color palette.
- **3-word personality**: Precise, calm, authoritative.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Refined minimalism with finance-inspired precision.
- **Theme**: Dark Sidebar (Rail) with Neutral (#F5EFE8) content surface.
- **Typography**: 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (currently in use), high contrast hierarchy.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Primary: #567C8D (Steel Blue)
  - Secondary: #C8D9E6 (Light Blue/Azure)
  - Tertiary/Sidebar: #2F4156 (Navy)
  - Neutral/Background: #F5EFE8 (Off-White)

### Design Principles
1. **Precision over Decoration**: Every pixel should serve a purpose; avoid unnecessary "AI slop" like side-stripe borders or glow effects.
2. **Layered Depth**: Use subtle shadows and clear rounding (24px) to create vertical hierarchy.
3. **Information Density**: Balance density with breathing room (generous white space in content, compact rail sidebar).
4. **Structured Anchor**: Layouts should feel grounded and aligned (40px content padding).
